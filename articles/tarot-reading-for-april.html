Tarot Horoscope for April 2019

Famous Tarot Reader Mita Bhan unfolds Tarot horoscope for the month of April 2019. Read on to find out what is in store for your sign this month. Consult Mita Bhan now on Astroyogi!

Aries

There’s something about you few can match. Maybe it’s the way you think so quickly on your feet. Or maybe it’s the way you’re determined to accomplish whatever is set before you. This month sees you shine both at the workplace and in your personal life. Your intelligence and perceptiveness will the respect of those around you. And if dare someone disagrees, they may get a taste of your famous temper as well. Enjoy this phase. Lots can be done. Lots can be said. And lots can be achieved. You’re on a roll, and it can only get better from here.

Taurus

Time to let go of the past and the pain dear Taurean. No point holding onto the memories when you’ve got a whole new life ahead of you. Forgive, forget and move on. New beginnings are at hand, and for those who’ve been broken-hearted, new love is in the cards. But first, you need to free up the space to let new, positive energies in. Go on and have a good cry if you need to. Friends and family will support you and inspire you in whatever you want to do. Pick up the pieces and resolve to take care of your future.

Gemini

Freedom at last. You may get the time out to do what you want. Or you may be liberated from an old fear or block that was holding you back all this while. Look ahead with courage and the belief that you can and will do it. A slow and sluggish month at work leaves you with more free time to get your personal life organized. A family trip may be in the offing which would be just what you need. If single, get ready for some excitement up ahead. Make way for the new burst of energy that’s about to come into your life.

Cancer

Temper dear Cancerian. If you’re not exploding at the drop of the hat. You may discover people around you are cranky, irritable or just plain annoying. Instead of letting up the energy build up, how about releasing all that pent up angry. Do something to destress yourself. At work, someone may get an unfair advantage over you. Or a competitor may act unfairly. You do have rivals out there, no point denying it. How about learning the art of cooperation.

Leo

You’ve been doing too much for too many people of late. And you may be feeling tired, pulled in every direction and completely scattered. Time to gather up your energies and learn to say No to those who are wasting your time or simply taking advantage of your generosity. Ask yourself if you’ve fallen in the trap of people pleasing and do something about it. A job change may be in the offing. And if you haven't updated your resume yet, maybe it’s time to bring in some changes in your life?

Virgo

You may be in denial about something which is obvious to the rest of the world. Time to be completely honest with yourself. Other people may start changing their behavior towards you. Soul searching will reveal where you may have done wrong. Make sure all the work’s done in time and in the correct way. A third party may cause trouble in your relationship or you may have to contend with some petty gossip. If you’ve been keeping to yourself for a while now, how about making an effort to get out and party before the invitations start drying up?

Libra

You’re at the crossroads. You may have to make an important decision about your life. Weigh the pros and cons and just go ahead and choose a path. The more you delay the more time you lose. A job opportunity may come your way which could be in another location or industry. Your long term mate may give you an ultimatum about committing to marriage. Or your parents may not feel like waiting for your excuses any longer. Time to pull up your socks and start doing what you promised to do.

Scorpio

Phew! The worst is over and things slowly begin to fall into place. The tensions seem to have subsided both at work and at home. If ill, the cards indicate slow but sure recovery. Have patience with people slower or younger than you. And if you were planning to buy a house or a car, things may get finalized. A long-standing issue with a loved one could clear up this week. Sudden gains are indicated midmonth plus some international travel.

Saggitarius

One of your wishes is coming true. Though not in the form you originally envisaged. Look forward to a month of harmony, peace, and absolute love. A good time to spend time with loved ones. The cards urge you to share your feelings with someone special. A hobby could turn into a moneymaking opportunity. And you may discover something very valuable in your own cupboard. Or find something you thought you had lost. Not a time to be by yourself, the cards urge you to go out and spread the cheer! 

Capricorn

What you want deep down is to make an impact, to do something no one else has ever dreamed of. And you know what, you can do it. Just take a little more of a realistic view of your surroundings and cultivate a little more patience. Be a little more assertive at work. You’ve got the makings of a leader. Just get out and show them. If something has been troubling you in your personal life, don’t keep quiet. Clear the air, say what’s in your mind honestly and discover a new sense of peace. The time is favorable for accomplishment and inner peace.

Aquarius

Whatever you want will be yours. Whatever you are working for will be realized. And whoever you want may want you back. The cards are all about setting clear goals, believing in your abilities and having faith in things working out in your favor. A good period for complete pending jobs. Your clear mind and assertiveness will ensure success in whatever you wish for. In terms of negotiations and legal issues, victory is yours.

Pisces

An opportunity may come your way to upgrade your skills. It may even be someone who knows a lot and takes on a mentoring role in your life. Keep your mind open to new experiences. And realize the value of this period. Someday soon you’ll be the one in power and you’ll have all these people to thank for taking you there. In relationships, a confusing period comes to an end. The person you’ve been eyeing for a while is interested. Who’s going to make the first move?


Mita Bhan
