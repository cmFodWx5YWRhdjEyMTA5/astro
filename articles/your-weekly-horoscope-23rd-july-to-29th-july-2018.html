Your Weekly Horoscope – 23rd July to 29th July 2018

Your weekly horoscope (23rd to 29th July, 2018) analysis can provide insights on the crucial planetary movements and its impact on your life. 

Aries
Aries, you aren’t known for thinking things through. Your reactions are sudden, rash, explosive. This week, you may make fast decisions relating to your career. This can be both a good and a bad thing. A speedy reaction may mean you are responding to your gut, which is a more accurate indication of your true feelings and desires, however, it can also mean that you plough ahead without truly thinking things through. It is advisable that you exercise a degree of caution even if you don’t want to! Your home life is harmonious, and your health is well. If expecting, good news is on the horizon. Children bring happiness into your life with a powerful immediacy. Your romantic life may need to be kick-started. You can do that, can’t you Aries?

Taurus
You might not be an obvious solitary sign like Scorpio or Capricorn, but Taurus natives are certainly drawn to alone time. You are able to appreciate the little things in life alone as your approach is patient, gentle. Spending time in nature undisturbed calls to you and will make you feel deeply fulfilled. You may have anxiety about moving to a new place and it’s important that you resolve this and make peace with any changes you are about to make. You may be quarrelsome this week due to in innate restlessness. Although patient and plodding like the bull, when you erupt you really let go. Ensure that you don’t take your volatile emotions out on those you hold dear. You are in your element when redecorating this week. Seeing a project reach completion satisfies you.

Gemini
Gemini’s are geniuses at making money (and spending it too.) There’s always some inventive way that you end up boosting your income. You know a little about a lot, and you know a great deal of people so it’s easy for you to find yourself propelled by those you know and what you know. For Gemini, it’s all about connections. This is a good period to invest what you’ve earned so that it can pay off for you in future. If you are interested in art and/or politics, you will reap especially good rewards. This week you feel active and sociable. You will be on true Gemini form. New things are coming your way, and this is exciting. Remember the saying ‘out with the old and in with the new.’ Think about what old things you are prepared to part with.

Cancer
Cancer, as an emotional sign you need a release valve to ensure that you get your emotions out in a constructive way. When your emotions are turned inwards, they can become almost defensive, making you too passive. Pouring your caring energy out into various courses strengthens and fortifies you. You may be lacking in confidence and this can lead to procrastination, delays in completing tasks and a build-up of work. If you can tackle things in bite sized pieces, you will feel less overwhelmed. Your mood may fluctuate but you can moderate this with meditation and exercise. Finding a way to self-sooth is vital. Financially, this is a great time for you and at work you will maintain a steady rhythm.

Leo
Leo, you are on the tail-end of some minor self-confidence issues and this can manifest in the form of temper tantrums, as Leo feels more comfortable with anger than with sadness. Not one to retreat and lick your wounds, you’ll go out all guns blazing. You should avoid hurting the feelings of those you care for by being inconsiderate and rash. You may have to say goodbye to someone you love deeply, and struggle with letting go. Meditation can help you embrace the fluctuating feelings. You are also still struggling with the feeling that there is a lack of love in your life, and it can be hard for you to know how to fill it. It’s important to remember that although love can come from many outside sources, you must also create a well of it within yourself.

Virgo
A Virgo with a confidence boost is far more productive. A little encouragement goes a long way with you. When you feel confident, you are poised for success as it’s easy for you to go after what you want directly and self-assuredly. You achieve success in every new project that you try, encouraging you to go after what you want. At work, you are appreciated for all that you do. Your health and financial status remain well, and new opportunities present themselves. You are in the position to pick and choose what you want, and you have a practical mind that is well-aware of how to make such decisions. Your mind may be distracted towards the end of the week and drawn towards matters of sex. Meditation may help.

Libra
Libra, a balance of hard work and luck will always get you where you need to be. You are personable and charming and people naturally like you. You are not one to rock the boat or be controversial, and that appeals to people. Some may be jealous of you as you seem to skate by in life on a combination of looks, charm and grace that not all possess. Don’t be deterred by this. Keep following your own road. This week you will enjoy a cordial, harmonious and peaceful time. If single, romance is in the air. You are attracted to refined partners who have a degree of sophistication and cultivation. Go after the partner of your dreams, why not?

Scorpio
Scorpio, you have entered a favourable phase in which luck supports everything you do. You have an enthusiasm for life that is infectious. It is during times like this that magic can happen. When we are in love with life and push forward into it with energy and intention, the universe parts like water around a kayak. You may notice that results from hard work may be delayed, but they are coming, so don’t be deterred. Your love life needs galvanising, and, on some level, you know this. Perhaps you’ve been ignoring the realm of relationships for some time out of fear or distraction. At work, you continue to feel excited, motivated and powerfully energised. You want to get things done, and you do. If a student, ensure that you revise and focus

Sagittarius
This week you enter into a slightly unfavourable phase and you are required to be more focused in your professional life and your personal life may feel mixed, some things going well and others less so. There is also the typical Sagittarius drive to explore and have adventures, and as such, you may plan a tour or long trip. You develop a keen interest in social activities and want to spread your wings. Just make sure that you are not burying deeper emotions in a constant stream of fun. You must sit up and face the music at some point. Ultimately, good fortune is promised, and this is a good period for every aspect of life. Professionally, this period is good although there is a degree of work pressure.

Capricorn
Capricorn, you always have the intention to expand everything you touch. Something is never enough as it is, it has to be grown, cultivated, encouraged, exaggerated. Whether it’s your career, a business or a hobby, you want to make the most of whatever you can. If coupled up, you may be thinking of marriage and luck will favour any romantic union you pursue. Others may envy how seemingly perfect your life appears to be. You may be sensitive to this, or self-conscious of it, but it’s important that you don’t start to sabotage yourself to make others feel better. You need to continue to be conscious of bad influences, as you are a sign that is straight-laced until you need to let off steam, then all bets are off. Don’t do anything that will trip you down the ladder.

Aquarius
At work, you continue to excel and reach your goals. Your particular brand of brilliance works well and pushes you on. In your personal life, things are also ticking along nicely. This is a great week for those of who are writers at heart. Ideas seem to come to you out of the blue, and writers block fades away. You are able to create work that seems ahead of its time or fresh and new in some way. This can lead to fame or money, if you have the consistency to work the idea to competition. Even if you are not a writer, professionally you can’t help but do well. There is a blissful feeling to all you do. It feels as though everything is well in the world, and that’s because it is.

Pisces
Happy news is on the horizon so be prepared for it. At work, you are drawn towards doing something new and innovative which may impress those around you, who may have considered you too passive to change up the status quo. You have the drive to innovate and may put plans into motion that have previously just been daydreaming. At work, things can be troublesome due to a feeling of competitiveness. Try to only compete with yourself and focus primarily on what you can do, rather than on what others are doing. For health reasons, try to eliminate sugar and processed foods. Eat well for balanced emotions and mental health. Life is smooth and harmonious so enjoy the ride.
