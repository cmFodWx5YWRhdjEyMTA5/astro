World Cup 2019 - Potential of each team to take the Cup

All the major teams participating in this year’s World Cup have announced their teams and each of these teams consists of the best 11 and by the looks of it, the 2019 World Cup is going to be one of the hardest tournaments ever played in the arena of Cricket. Each of the major teams has some very strong players ever played and the chances to which team might take the cup can hardly be decided at the moment but to indicate the chances of each of the teams, Expert Astrologer from Astroyogi has brought to you a thorough insight of the major 6 teams:

This year a total of 10 teams will participate in the epic battle of Cricket and put forth their candidacy to win the 12th World Cup title. The teams playing in this year’s World cup are -

  • England

  • India

  • Australia

  • New Zealand

  • South Africa

  • Sri Lanka

  • West Indies

  • Pakistan

  • Bangladesh

  • Afghanistan

All of the 10 teams have arrived in England and the practice matches are in session. While it would be great to see some of these teams with improved performance and a better opposition to play with, mentioned below is the comparison of the major 6 teams that can have a greater chance at winning. Read on to know more about the 6 strongest teams and their potential to take the cup based on the zodiac signs of their captains.

The top 5 teams of the 2019 World Cup -

Either former winners or the best performers in the previous World Cup, these 5 teams give a promising outlook for this year’s tournament. The top 5 include England, India, Australia, South Africa, and New Zealand can surely be seen battling it out in the final match and before that happens they deserve a look over astrologically for you to have a better idea about them all.

Australia in the 2019 World Cup

The current holder of the World Cup and one of the most prominent teams of the cricketing world, the Aussies are going to start off their journey in the tournament playing against Afghanistan. The 2015 champions have gambled their way with a lot of new faces, more bowlers than batsmen and two all-rounders. Captain Aaron Finch will make sure they take the trophy yet again with strong players like David Warner, Steve Smith and Maxwell by his side.

Astrological Analysis of Aaron Finch

Born on 17th November 1986, the sun sign of Finch is Scorpio and his name sign based on the first letter of his name becomes Aries. Both signs have their ruling planet to be Mars. Looking at his birth chart, Moon is present in the 5th house indicating a lot of ups and downs for the Aussie captain. The conjugation of Mars with Rahu is also indicating poor times for him. Although the Australian team is a strong contender in this year’s World Cup, the positioning of stars does not indicate an easy ride for them.

England in the 2019 World Cup

The host of the mega championship, England have managed to get a balanced squad with many new faces who have proven their performance in different formats. Eion Morgan is going to be the captain of the English team and this will be his first World Cup captaincy. The team has been selected in the typical format with more batsmen like Ben Stokes, Joe Root than bowlers and a few all-rounders, Moeen Ali is one of them. It will be refreshing to see a majority of young blood on the field playing for their country.

Astrological Analysis of Eion Morgan

Captain Eion Morgan was born on 10th September 1986 and based on this his sun sign becomes Virgo and his name sign becomes Aries as his name starts with E. According to his sun sign Moon is present in the 7th house and the ruling planet Mercury is in the house of fortune. These circumstances give positive indications for the England team in the upcoming matches. If we talk about the name sign of Morgan, then the predictions give a different story, the outcome of Rahu and Mars in the 3rd house do not give good results for the team, however, conjugation of Saturn and Ketu might help him out.

India in the 2019 World Cup

The Indian team is definitely one of the strongest and with the IPL season just concluded, they are all in form. Following up on this form, young and new shoulders have been given the responsibility to lead India to victory for this year. Virat is going to lead the team along with everyone’s favorite and most loved MSD by his side. He has been given a strong team with 5 bowlers, 4 all-rounders and a stunning list of wicketkeepers. A strong team indeed! Hitman Sharma will open for the team and his partnership with Shikhar Dhawan has proven to be fruitful throughout the years. Bowlers like Kuldeep Yadav and Yuzvendra Chahal will have a lot to prove with their fresh talent while Bhuvaneshwar Kumar and Jasprit Bumrah will try to maintain their performance for better results.

Astrological Analysis of Virat Kohli

Kohli was born on 5th November 1988 and his sun sign is Scorpio. Based on the first letter of his name, his name sign becomes Taurus. Basing the predictions on his sun sign, the condition of the Indian team is quite similar to that of Australia but if we consider his name sign then the circumstances give a positive outcome. Ruling planet Venus is present in the 12th house and is creating beneficial situations for the Indian squad. Overall, a good performance is expected of them.

South Africa in the 2019 World Cup

With Faf du Plessis leading the team, it is going to be a power-packed year for the South Africans. The team has managed to find a balance between the batsmen and bowlers and picked the best of them all. New players have also been given a shot based on their performance in different formats. Continuing their form throughout the IPL season, Imran Tahir and Kagiso Rabada have earned their spot and their bowling can be a game-changer for the team. De Kock is also in good form and Duminy being an all-rounder shall too provide better outcomes for the SA team.

Astrological Analysis of Faf du Plessis

Birthdate of Faf du Plessis is 13th July 1984, making Cancer his sun sign and his name sign becomes Sagittarius. The ruling planet of Cancer, Moon is present in the house of fortune and indicates good outcomes for the team. Talking about his name sign, the positioning of the Moon is indicated to be in the 6th house. All of these are giving positive results for the South African team.

New Zealand in the 2019 World Cup

The current form of all the Kiwi players has earned them a spot on our list of top teams in the run for this year’s trophy. The team of New Zealand has seen the little variation for this year and has a good combination of batsmen and bowlers by their side. Kane Williamson is the Captain and Ross Taylor along with Martin Guptill will give him the right support he needs to take his team all the way to the finals. All-rounders are all new faces with good performance going on for them and the list of bowlers looks promising too. The squad has a total of 3 wicket-keeper batsman and it will be interesting to see how this plays out for them.

Astrological Analysis of Kane Williamson

Date of birth of Kane Williamson is 8th August 1990 which makes his sun sign to be Leo. The name sign of his becomes Gemini. Looking from the perspective of his sun sign, Moon is present in the 8th house and Mars is present along with Rahu. All of these indicate mixed results for the Kiwi team. Name sign suggests that the team might need to sweat it out a little more than normal if they wish to earn the top spot.

Based on the above analysis, India, Australia, and South Africa might make it to the finals and if they do, it will be a treat for the cricket fans.
