Your Weekly Horoscope – 29 Oct to 4 Nov 2018

Your weekly horoscope ( 29th Oct to 4th Nov, 2018 ) analysis can provide insights on the crucial planetary movements and its impact on your life. Stay tuned to Astroyogi’s expert astrologers for horoscope analysis.

Aries
Known as a feisty, fiery sign, this energy is maximised this week due to the continuing impact of the sun in Libra. Your tempestuous energy is likely to manifest with friends and family, the closer the person is to you, the more of your heat they will experience! If single and dating, this energy manifests slightly differently and can actually make you quite an exciting and inciting catch. It’s an usual blend of energy, but those who have known you a lot time may not be so appreciative! At work, you are enthusiastic, capable and fun, making you an asset. There is a buzz and hum of romantic energy surrounding you whether you choose to pursue it or not. Do take care of your mental and physical health. Be consistent with your practices.

Taurus
You need to be patient and you also need to apply consistency to all your dealings. Not everything will be going as planned. This can be frustrating for you, but you are typically a patient sign, although once that famous temper blows, you might as well be standing in the shadow of Mount Vesuvius! Things may progress slowly, but even a slow speed is momentum of some kind and should be appreciated. You may experience unnecessary conflicts with women and you may also be prone to aches and pains in the body. Stretching, exercise, meditation and yoga can all assist, especially if, as we say, they are practiced consistently. You’ll be looking to grow or process in same way, a great internal stretch may be taking place.

Gemini
You are at your best when you embrace your inner child. When you are joyful, curious, enthusiastic and innately happy, you draw people to you effortlessly. Of course, there is a flip side to your agreeable childishness. You may be somewhat self-involved or silly. Try to bring the best of yourself to the table and remember, childlike, not childish, is the way forward. You may re-connect with people from your past who meant a lot to you. Gemini’s love connections of all kinds so you’ll appreciate this. You impress others and perform well at work and in your studies making this a good time to learn and/or push yourself generally. Be open to the abundance of love that is coming your way. Welcome it with open arms.

Cancer
Cancer is very much in touch with maternal energy. At its best, this energy materialises as a soothing, warm, protective, tenaciousness. At it’s worst, or when out of balance, it comes out as cloying, controlling and limiting. Make sure that you utilise the very best of this energy as much as you can. In fact, Cancer is often best pouring its nurturing, nourishing energy inward first, filling its own cup before pouring to others. This way you avoid little resentments that build up. Life may require that you replan certain things that seemed like sure wins. This isn’t a bad thing necessarily, just a redirection. The future may worry you and you might feel unsettled. Focus on positivity and remember, we can all only ever control the here and now. This should empower you.

Leo
You continue to be active and social, living life to the nth degree. Avoid burning yourself out but do enjoy feeling like a true Leo. All eyes will be on you, and that’s just how you like it. You enjoy spending time with friends and family. You simply want to be with people. Your sunny disposition awards you many friends. You will also make plenty of new friends due to your fun, larger than life character, you are in a romantic mood and if you have a partner on the scene, you will be gregarious and generous. If you have the opportunity to take a short trip, you should do so, as this will revitalise you. Be cautious of your health. There are no severe conditions indicated here, but you may want to be mindful of your skin and throat, which can be indicative of deeper issues. Take time to connect with yourself at the end of each day. Self-care is paramount.

Virgo
You will make a financial profit this week, but you may still feel as though you just aren’t saving or earning enough. Of course, a few conscious financial decisions could change all this. This is a wonderful time to plan for your future. it is important that you watch what you say – you may needlessly offend, which will not be your intention. Misunderstandings and impatience are likely, especially with those with whom you are close. Although you might feel like hibernating, it’s important that you push yourself out and get active with other people. This will stop you from falling into a low mood from which you may find it difficult to climb out of. Your well-calibrated mind can be an enemy at times. Communication is the key to your success – speak honestly and diplomatically for results. Avoid negative habits and pastimes.

Libra
You start this week feeling not quite yourself. You may feel lost in a maze of indecision or be struggling to concentrate on something that matters immensely to you. You may also be perturbed about loss. In truth, loss is a part of life. Think about the trees of autumn that shed their leaves to make way for new ones. It is an inevitable part of life and changing our perspective of this, and thus coming to terms with it, is an essential part of every person’s journey. You must not cling too tightly to any one thing, enable things to ebb and flow, come and go. Trust that the important things, and people, will stay. Dreams may come true this week so don’t be afraid to ask the universe for what you long for. Home life is harmonious. If you are going to spend money on anything, do it on your own health and beautification. This will make you happy.

Scorpio
Scorpio, you always want a change. You hate when things become too stagnant, and sometimes will shake things up just for the sake of it. It’s important to differentiate between change which is evolving you and change for changes sake. Be wise in your decision making, and if you cannot be wise, halt. Proceed with caution. Wait until things are better. Life is not as serious as a strategic game of chess, but sometimes there are wrong moves to make. Jealousy may prompt you to want to underperform. You don’t like the spotlight and may shirk from it if it begins to impose on you, especially in negative ways. Do not let the ill intensions of others dissuade you from your path. Own your glory. Something new is on the cards in love and/or in your family life. This may be a change you can get on board with.

Sagittarius
This is a great time for love. If part of a couple, you feel supported, understood and treasured. You flourish under these circumstances. If there have been issues that have arisen between you and your partner, you are able to tackle them head on and resolve them. This is a great time for conflict resolution. Remember, respecting one another’s opinions, values and differences is an essential part of any relationship. If single, you will be immensely attractive to prospective new suitors, seemingly without trying. Plan for the future but do so calmly not frantically. Allow things to unravel. You will enjoy indulging in the finer things in life, such as food and travel. Try to reign yourself in as you are prone to excess. Your joyous, expansive spirit understands no boundary, which can be a good thing, until we get to the consequences! There may be some pressure at work, but nothing you can’t manage, not with your sunny spirit.

Capricorn
A theme in the lives of all Capricorns is work. You work harder than most, and as a result, you are often more successful too. You are not afraid of competition, because you focus on your own path. Others may inspire, they may even encourage you to speed up, but they can’t make you anxious or afraid. You understand your own worth. For the average Capricorn, finding value beyond work is the important lesson. You are more important that what you do and what you earn, but do you always understand that? There are many opportunities coming your way at work and in the world at large. Be open and say yes. You need to balance the many different facets of your life, and this may require constant tinkering and rejigging, but once mastered you will be immensely rewarded by a life that looks and feels like a right fit. Avoid bad influences. Avoid smoking, drinking, gambling and other insidious habits that can eat away at your success.

Aquarius
Health is true weather. We can’t accomplish much without it. As well as physical health, mental and emotional health is so important. Aquarius is the sign of genius and eccentricity, and as such, your mental health needs to be consistently cared for. This doesn’t mean that you are more prone to mental illness than anyone else, but that you need to check in with your mental landscape frequently. Our thoughts control our actions and therefore our life’s so it’s important to understand how you are thinking and making changes if necessary. As a fixed sign, your dig your heels in when it comes to life’s changes and challenges, of which there will be some this week. There are plenty of auspicious events for you to partake in, giving you a new lease of life. Overall, this is a good week for Aquarius natives so enjoy! 

Pisces
Pisces, as a water sign, you are temperamental by your very natural. You ebb and flow like a lake, sometimes tranquil and calm and other times tempestuous and aggrieved. You experience life as a serious of emotional responses. This can make certain moments quite magical but can also plunge you deep into the current of despair if you are not adequately prepared. Pisces often needs direction, and if unable to direct itself, requires a map from others. Make sure that you entrust this role to trustworthy people who have your best interests at heart. This is not something to give away lightly. Your love life is smooth and harmonious with that glimmer of sweet delight that Pisces brings to the table. Financially, you are in a stable position and professionally, things are ticking along well.
