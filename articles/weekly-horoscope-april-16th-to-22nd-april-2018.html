Weekly Horoscope – April 16th to 22nd April 2018

Your weekly horoscope (16th April to 22nd April, 2018) analysis can provide insights on the crucial planetary movements and its impact on your life. Consult Astroyogi’s expert astrologers for personalised horoscope analysis.

Aries
With the sun continuing in your own sign of Aries, confidence is certainly not an issue. You’ll throw yourself into life headfirst and be a true creator of your own destiny. Aries is the first sign of the zodiac and it has an innate longing to be first in all facets of life. Aries wants to win, and it has the confidence and sincerity of spirit to do just that. Aries is also honest about it’s intentions. There’s a childlike sense of wonder to natives belonging to this sign. The impact of Mercury in Aquarius draws you to travel, social activity and eccentric people. You are especially attracted to individuals and those that break the mould. Venus moves into slow and plodding Taurus giving you the opportunity to sit down and smell the roses in love and express gratitude for what you have. In fact, your love life becomes enchanted and enthralling.

Taurus
This week, the sun in Aries take life in a slightly different direction. We are moving away from the energy and influence of Pisces and towards a more ‘Aries’ flavour. Ultimately, Aries energy works well for Taurus natives, it lends you a sense of gusto and get up and go. Aries brings an urgency to all things, but unlike Gemini restlessness for instance, which is more about the mind, Aries brings physical action so things get started (if not always completed.) But this is where your Taurus tenacity comes in – you do see things through to the end in a methodical sense. Beware of minor irritation to the eyes. Otherwise, your health is well. Things continue to go well at work so this is an area in which you can relax. Venus moves from Aries to Taurus and you are utterly devoted in love. Likewise, your partner shows you love and care.

Gemini
With the sun now in Aries, you are in an absolutely brilliant place. Aries energy is so in sync with Gemini. You feel so confident and in high jovial spirits. You feel courageous, ready to take on the world. This is the placement of ideas and action working cohesively together and you may feel incredibly excited at your ability to will your wildest dreams into being. There is a sense that nothing can stop you, you are a dynamo of energy. You are also very enthusiastic and driven in love which is exciting and arousing to potential new partners. You have a true zest for life which is remarkably infectious. With Venus moving from Aries to Taurus, you may experience a shift in gears in terms of love. You will go from a playful, friendly approach to love, to something more romantic, spiritual and profound.

Cancer
With the sun in Aries, you’ve had a taste of success and now you want more. People are so impressed with you at work that this success is guaranteed especially now that you have demonstrated that you have a thirst to continue. Half of the battle is wanting to be there, and you seem to have that half covered. You have never been more at your best at work and this is set to continue this week. It’s not even a conscious effort on your part, just a natural phenomenon. This week, Venus moves from Aries to Taurus on the 20th so your love life will experience a slight shift. Venus in Taurus encourages your partner to provide you with positive energy which is tremendously nourishing and also a huge relief. Whatever else is going on, your partner is supportive.

Leo
With the sun in Aries, you are moving from a place of tension and low self-confidence to one of luck and good fortune. You may benefit from an inheritance and you also have an over-arching interest in spirituality which helps you to understand yourself and your place in the grand scheme of life. We all have a mission or purpose and finding it is half of the battle. Still, you seem to be on the way there. Your personal life proceeds smoothly and your romantic partner is supportive of you. You may meet a famous person! Be sure to get their autograph. Don’t ignore any health issues which may arise. Make sure that you check in with yourself and how you feel. As Venus moves from Aries to Taurus, you forge new romantic allegiances and also good friends. A new love may enter your life rather unexpectedly.

Virgo
The impact of the sun in Aries asks you to look at your health, both physically and mentally. Remember, that we must take a holistic approach to our health. It is possible that you are suffering with low self-esteem and also that you are feeling physically unwell. You may also lack concentration. It is important that you change your environment to one that is relaxing, peaceful and ideally, close to nature. This is the ideal environment for healing. Practice self-nurturing and remove anything from your life that does not bring you well-being, happiness, use or pleasure. This is a good time to throw out the old which no longer serves you. With Venus moving into Taurus, you may experience some physical discomfort and may overindulge which can lead to plumpness. Remember that meditation and yoga are your allies when it comes to health.

Libra
As the sun moves into Aries, problems may emerge between you and your life partner. As a result of this, other parts of your life may be affected. As a sign that desires balance, this may be very uncomfortable for you. The stars seem to suggest that a big trip will right any wrongs, so if you are in a position to take a trip, please do so. You may feel somewhat unromantic, but romance is a flame that can be rekindled. Taking a step back from life temporarily can make you feel much better. You truly need to take time for yourself. With Venus in Taurus, it is possible that you may enter parenthood. There may be some unresolved tension in your love life.

Scorpio
With the sun moving into Aries, you may experience a life-changing event. Your personal and marital life will also be prosperous. Other than a few minor setbacks, you will experience perfect health. You continue to spend quality time with family and at home. It is important that home generates the right mood and feeling for you, as this is where you go to feel safe. If you need to redecorate or make some changes, ensure that you do. With Venus moving from Aries, where it created some unstable effects, to Taurus, you’ll find that something new will come into your home. This is also a time where plenty of love and affection will be received from your mother. If you are a mother, you are in a position to bestow love.

Sagittarius
On the 14th April, the sun moved into Aries and the impact is that you may become famous or at least recognised in your local community. Not bad news, is it? Happiness will come to you via education and through children, whether you teach or parent them. In your professional life, success is guaranteed. There is a sense that fate is supporting all that you do, which is a marvellous way to feel. It may seem that everything is falling perfectly into place. You are also in a romantic mood and are enjoying spending time with your partner. As Venus moves into Taurus on the 20th, you will take many successful short trips. It may sound cliché, but there may be a sense of ‘finding yourself’ and fate will support you.

Capricorn
With the sun now in Aries, a change is afoot in both your professional and personal life and close friends will support you as these changes hit. Fortunately, these changes aren’t negative. It may be that you are promoted or find a new partner. Anyway, life is designed to change and move. It is a natural part of the world we live in and learning to move and adapt with it is vital. None of us were born for stasis. You may be less focused at work so try to apply yourself. If you are pursuing your education, you will continue to do well. You truly shine. As Venus moves from Aries to Taurus on the 20th, you find yourself in a financially strong position and feeling very harmonious. Remember that hard work will improve your bank balance.

Aquarius
With the sun in Aries, you will be proactive. Aries is the go-getter of the zodiac and it simply doesn’t wait around, especially for things to come to it! Even if you tend to sit back and wait in life, this just won’t be the case this week. You will want to get things done, move, be active and you will be ready to get those brilliant ideas of yours out into the world. You are supportive of those younger than you and they support you in turn. You also feel enthusiastic and opportunistic as the world opens itself to you. You feel like a kid at a buffet. There are just endless treats to feast on. As Venus transits into Taurus from Aries, you will find that serious issues become resolved and this sense of resolution naturally brings closure. The women in your life become especially important to you and take on valued significance. Your love life is enhanced in a way that is pure magic. In love, things may be moving along.

Pisces
This is a great week for Pisces natives as you start the week in a financially strong state. You are learning to save and treat your money with care. You also have high self-confidence which motivates you to make change. You can thank Aries for the spring in your step! A change in career is indicated and this is a wonderful period for romance. You long to travel with your partner so why not make this happen? With family, there is a sense of harmony. Venus moves from Aries to Taurus on the 20th and you may spend more than you really need to. Try to get back to those good saving habits. Your attitude will be rather laidback, and your personal life will run smoothly and peacefully.
