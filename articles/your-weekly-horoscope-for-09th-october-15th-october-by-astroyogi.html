Your Weekly Horoscope For 09th October - 15th October By astroYogi

Your weekly horoscope predictions by the expert astrologers of astroYogi, read on. Consult our experts online for a personalised horoscope analysis and guidance.

Aries : Your strong fit constitution can come under attack from your own zest for life. As you throw yourself into life full pelt, you may bare the cuts and scrapes of your interactions. Take care of your body this week, dear Aries. On the 13th, Mercury moves into Libra and you need to exercise caution when it comes to your words. You appreciate honesty and say what you mean but sometimes the truth can sound blunt, crass and quite simply unappealing, especially on more sensitive ears. Soften your words and remember that sometimes being kind is more important than being right, especially if you are quarreling with a partner or family member. Venus is now in Virgo and this can slow down the infamous speed cars that Aries natives are. When we slow down, we can reflect and take stock and this is an important thing for you to do. As you race through life you live it fully, but sometimes looking back reveals important truths.

Taurus : The sun remains in Virgo and you are definitely feeling famous in some way. It may not be the Hollywood fame (the kind the world has become accustomed to) but it may be local fame or fame within your community. Your words carry weight and people look to you for influence and action. Mercury is moving from Virgo to Libra creating a time of transition that you will feel this week. Mercury in Libra lends you all of its artistic, beautiful qualities. For those of you who work in the media or performing arts, your skills are heightened. You are known for having a melodious, soothing voice (this is one of the hallmarks of Taurus as it is a sign ruled by Venus which promotes beauty) and your voice takes you a long way this week. As Venus moves from Leo to Virgo, you can expect to receive good news. Those of you looking for a new job will find it and cash effortlessly flows into your life. All areas of life feel good and satisfying. This is a good week for you dear Taurus.

Gemini : You are a whirlwind Gemini and despite your happiness at home, you want change. Are you thinking about your career and have you taken steps to make that change yet? The sun remains in Virgo so career should still be your focus, at least, this is what is indicated by the stars. On the 13th, Mercury moves into fellow air sign Libra and your personal life is put into focus. This may be a time of conflict and arguments. You may worry and feel generally anxious or low. As an air sign, you are often in your head and when your thoughts become negative, this impacts your mood and health. It is important to try to disconnect from your head. This is not easy for air signs as you feel deeply connected to your mind. To you, your thoughts are who and what you are. But is that true? A bad mood doesn’t always mean that something is wrong. How we think about it makes all the difference. Meditation or some creative task will help you use your mind constructively. You will then see that the mind is something that works for you and is only a part of you. Venus in Virgo suggests an exciting new love. Are you ready to embrace it?

Cancer : Just like the animal that represents you, the crab, Cancer natives love their homes but they also like to wander. Note that the crab carries its home on its back. This is an important metaphor for how Cancer’s like to live. They want to move and explore but home must always be close. In this way, there is something about the Sagittarius in the Cancer natives. On the 13th, Mercury moves into Libra and you may enjoy a huge profit or inheritance. You will have low energy. As a water sign, and as an emotional sign, your energy level tends to be dictated by your moods. Low mood equals low energy. Your approach is one that ebbs and flows like the tides that carry the crabs. If you’re feeling tired, take time to eat hearty foods, watch your iron intake, sleep and meditate. Your energy levels may then rebuild and stabilize. You want to spend time with friends doing low-key activities and this is to be encouraged. With Venus in Virgo, you actively spend time with younger siblings and take many short trips with them. Possibly you will consider a family of your own.

Leo : With the Sun in Virgo, you continue to benefit financially this week and you have a certain sense of savvy when it comes to investment that enables you to earn your money back (and then some profits.) Your relationships with relatives steadily improve. If there have been misunderstandings or family conflicts, this falls away this week. Your week is peppered with auspicious events and you do enjoy being the life and soul. On the 13th, Mercury moves into Libra and it’s essential that you keep tabs on your financial comings and goings. You may need to have a rethink to see how you can make things better. It’s possible that you are overspending in certain areas when you really don’t need to. You need to spend more time with your partner. You may be physically around them often but quantity does not equal quality. Venus is now in Virgo and you may find that your throat and skin are extra sensitive. Skin tends to show toxins and the throat can be connected to how we communicate and whether we feel we are being true to our own voice. Is any of this true for you?

Virgo : With the sun remaining in Virgo, this remains a week of growth and progression for you. It may be that you improving your health at the gym, completing a novel, advancing your career or revising for exams. Whatever you are focusing on, you are making great strides. On the 13th, Mercury moves into Libra and you are set to make some big financial gains, especially if you dabble in property. This is a wonderful phase for students and you will get your head down with your studies and do well. Your relatives may benefit you in some way. Perhaps they will assist you financially or give you good advice. With Venus in your own sign of Virgo, you may find that you consumed with thoughts about romance and love. You are seeking out passion. It is important to remember that sex is a natural wholesome act and that your desire for it is part of human nature. That said, it is better to find a partner whom you trust and admire. Be cautious that your desires do not lead you to make bad decisions. Understand that passion can come into our lives in many ways and forms.

Libra : With the sun remaining in Virgo, this week continues to bring a degree of mental restlessness and tension. It may be very hard for you to separate yourself from your thoughts. Meditation can assist as it will teach you to let thoughts come and go more freely, without attempting to analyse them at all times. Watch thoughts as you would watch birds in the sky. Let them pass. Let go. On the 13th, Mercury moves into Libra and business expansion is on your mind. How can you really push your business and make it a success? You are also more interested in spiritual matters and God. Certain practices can bring you closer to God such as meditation, yoga and other spiritual endeavours. This part of you will be most active and will be a source of great positivity for you. As Venus remains in Virgo, you may notice that life is becoming a tad more expensive. Is this necessary? Can you cut back on some spending? Perhaps money spent on clothes or trinkets? It is always a good idea to spend your money on your health. This is one area in which money is never wasted.

Scorpio : A self-confident Scorpio is a true marvel and this week, with the sun continuing in Virgo, you remain on top form. Thanks to your fixed and focused nature, you are able to make tremendous progress. You have not scattered this energy. Instead you have used it to its fullest. On the 13th October, Mercury moves into Libra and you’ll find that it may be difficult to save your money. There always seems to be some outgoing or another. Although life does throw some obstacles at you, you feel laid-back, calm and collected. This puts you in a strong position. You can tackle anything that comes your way. Indeed, this is the best way to deal with such things. Ultimately, it is important that you do not make any major decisions this week other than those which you have already made and continue to work on. New decisions should be held off until Mercury leaves Libra in November. With Venus in Virgo, love, money and possibly a new baby are on the horizon. If a student, you may need to work a little harder to get the results you desire. Use your Scorpion focus! 

Sagittarius : Like fellow signs Aries and Leo, you like to throw caution to the wind. For you, life is an adventure and to live it safely would be missing the point. You give more sensible signs heart attacks! Can’t you hear Virgo fainting in the background? This week, with the sun in Virgo, you are being schooled about what it means to be sensible. There’s a lot of pressure at work and how you manage it is very important. On the 13th October, Mercury moves into Libra enabling you to complete auspicious tasks. The attitude that you have which is one of passion and pushing for success enables you to do well and achieve desired targets. Although you are driven to succeed, you are also spending time with your partner and enjoying your time together. Venus is now in Virgo and this is a good placement for professionals. Although there is still a degree of pressure, you are weathering it well and gaining valuable skills. This doesn’t mean that you are abandoning your inner adventurer. It is just that you know that at certain times, other things need to come first.

Capricorn : Lucky Capricorn, with the sun in Virgo, luck remains on your side. Obstacles come your way, after all, nothing comes easily, at least not all the time, but you clear all obstacles effortlessly. This helps build your confidence as you know that you are perfectly capable. You may appear cool as a cucumber but this doesn’t mean that you don’t worry. You are just better at concealing it than most. On the 13th, Mercury moves into Libra and you continue to develop at work. It’s easy for you to accept challenges, although do be cautious about piling too much on your plate. You don’t need to prove anything to anyone, not even yourself. Others are highly supportive of you. They know that you are destined for greatness. With Venus in Virgo, you need to do your best to resist bad influences. You have a strong moral and ethical constitution. You believe in doing the right thing and this is very engrained in you. You also have awesome willpower. The problems come when you try to reward yourself after a period of challenging work. Your perception of how to treat yourself becomes skewed and you can fall into unhealthy habits. Perhaps the best approach is to aim for balance.

Aquarius : Are you meditating? Practicing yoga? When you get busy, you may want to ditch these practices. They may feel like too much of a drain on your time and yet they make such a powerful difference to your wellbeing. Try to keep up with your practice. Consistency is key. It will do you the world of good. On the 13th, Mercury moves from Virgo to Libra and your interest in spiritual matters is heightened. The spiritual realm is incredibly healing and will benefit you greatly. This is a wonderful time for love and marriage but it is important that you do not make any big decisions this week. Enjoy being in love and the sense of progression but don’t finalise anything. With Venus in Virgo, you need to exercise caution in love and at work. The best way to do this is to take your time. You are very good at getting a solid read on people so it is rare for anyone to pull one over on you. If you can trust in your own gut responses, this will guide you well in all areas of life.

Pisces : Spiritual Pisces, this is an interesting time for you. You are working through your ego and how this may be affecting different aspects of your life. This is a good goal to achieve and it is a work in progress throughout this life and the sooner you start, the more effective you will be at tackling minor irritants that arise. On the 13th October, Mercury moves into Libra bringing with it unexpected monetary gains. At work, it is easy for you to achieve all tasks that you have set yourself and this is recognised and acknowledged by fellow employees and superiors. This may give you the credibility to build your way up in the company. Venus continues in Virgo keeping life smooth and stable which suits you well. In love, you feel harmonious, deeply connected and blissfully happy. Your personality is one that ebbs and flows with the tide and as such a stable life is very beneficial for you. Some routine and stability helps your emotional and mental ups and downs.
