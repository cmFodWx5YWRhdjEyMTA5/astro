Weekly Horoscope For October 23th to 29th 2017 by astroYogi

Your weekly horoscope analysis can provide insights on the crucial planetary movements and its impact on your life. Stay tuned to astroYogi’s expert astrologers for horoscope analysis.

Aries Weekly Horoscope
With the sun remaining in Libra, all is not quiet on the domestic front. You may be prone to irritation and quarrels with loved ones due to a frenetic and underlying sense of frustration. Remember that your sign of Aries gives you the ability to directly assert yourself so there is no need for passive-aggression or explosive tantrums. Interestingly, this energy adds a spark to your love life and you may draw many to you as moths are drawn to a flame due to a promise of passion. Mercury remains in Libra and you need to watch your tongue as you may say things in the heat of the moment that you do not mean. Your home atmosphere may be affected by your temperamental moods so be aware of utilising positive outlets such as exercise to calm yourself down. With Venus in Virgo until the 1st November, you may feel in low spirits. It’s hard for you to tolerate feeling down in the dumps as you like to burn brightly. What do you love doing? Do more of it. It will help.

Taurus Weekly Horoscope
For Taurus natives, love is all-consuming. A steady love life helps you to feel content in this world. This week, with the sun in Libra, you become aware that you have been neglecting the special person in your life or your love life in general due to distraction or habit. Can you think of ways to brighten up your relationships? Things may happen slowly this week rather than with any sense of speed. You are a patient sign so this won’t bother you too much. With Mercury in Libra, a promising period for those in media or performing arts is guaranteed. Your health and finances are better than ever and you feel fit as a fiddle and full of energy. You feel that you can tackle and take on anything and there is a certain zest for life in all that you do. With Venus remaining in Virgo good news is coming your way. What it is totally depends on what’s going on in your life. Exciting times lay ahead! 

Gemini Weekly Horoscope
Gemini, you always manage to retain the essence of childhood even as you age. You have the gift of understanding the young and keeping elements of youth that remain important whatever our age such as curiosity and the believe that anything is possible. With the sun in Libra, children reward you by bringing you good news. There is also a chance that someone important from your past will come back into your life. Whether you decide to entertain the past or not is entirely up to you. If old friends or flames come knocking, it may be worth seeing if there is space for them in the life you have established now. Mercury in Libra may trigger a series of failed or delayed plans. The nervier side of you will be agitated by this but your spontaneous side may thrive on change. With Venus in Virgo, love will feature in a big way this week. It may come in the form of an old lover who finds their way back into your life or it may be someone new.

Cancer Weekly Horoscope
As an emotional sign, you feel your way through the world and are as resilient as they come. That said, this week you may feel stressed and tense when it comes to business opportunities. You may feel frustrated that things aren’t happening in the way that you imagined or quite as quickly as you’d planned. Can you seek expert advice and help from friends? Love fuels and feeds you so remember that you can always turn to loved ones for security. With Mercury in Libra there is the opportunity to profit from an inheritance or property but energetically you feel lethargic and low. For Cancer, your energy is tied up in how you feel. If you don’t feel like doing something, you don’t. You can try to push past this by motivating yourself with rewards. With Venus in Virgo you want to spend time with younger siblings and may take them out for many short trips. Family, as always, is important to you.

Leo Weekly Horoscope
With the sun in Libra, this week you are very active socially and physically. Your energy levels oscillate between lazy like a lion and full of power and vigour. This week requires a lot of energy from you as you travel, explore, keep social commitments and generally galivant. Keep your energy levels up with plenty of rest and nourishing foods. This week life feels fun and that is what you live for! There’s a hum of excitement in love that may feel like something from a movie or story book. With Mercury in Libra you need to keep an eye on your finances. You will likely be spending more than usual as for you fun usually is connected to finances! With Venus in Virgo, you may notice that your savings have taken a bit of a dip. If you can add some balance to your life you can enjoy saving as well as spending. It’s just making sure that you don’t become all or nothing in your approach. You may notice some issues relating to your throat or skin. Drink warming teas and eat well.

Virgo Weekly Horoscope
Virgo, you’ll be pleased to hear that with the sun in Libra, you’ll see some financial profit this week. You are encouraged to invest as much as you can and to build on your savings. As an earth sign, having a financial safety net makes you feel content and gives you the freedom and opportunity to take risks that you otherwise might not take. This week is a good time to think towards the future and to plan your next steps. Whether you focus on your career or love life, you can take action now to get things in motion. With Mercury in Libra, you will definitely see a financial gain, especially if you deal with properties. Those of you who are studying will excel with your keen minds and sharp focus. Success is certain. With Venus in Virgo, you may feel that your mind begins to wander. A famous saying states that ‘the devil makes work for idle hands.’ The best thing to do is to keep yourself busy either with a task, project or hobby until this period passes.

Libra Weekly Horoscope
Libra, with the sun in your own sign this week, you may have some difficulty concentrating. This is because Libra tends to flip-flop back and forth and over-analyse. You can teach yourself to be more decisive. You are a cerebral sign and you live in your mind. You need to learn how to respond to life with your gut instincts at times. Not every decision needs to be thought through so meticulously. In fact, some things don’t need to be thought through at all. Those who are older and more experienced than you might be able to assist you with this problem so please do seek them out. With Mercury in Libra, you are thinking of expanding your business and why not? You have the leadership skills and charisma to make this work. Use your diplomatic nature to avoid fiery altercations and arguments. With Venus in Virgo you are advised to spend money on your health and well-being. Focus on the things that make you feel good.

Scorpio Weekly Horoscope
With the sun in Libra, you may feel less confident than usual and as such you may doubt your ability to make sound decisions. If this is the case for you, seek the help of experts and those with relevant experiences. You are a highly evolved soul but even so guidance can still be useful for you. Mercury in Libra can suggest obstacles but you thrive on challenge and your mentality reminds laidback and not particularly affected. You should avoid making important life decisions at this time unless you can take stock of advice from trusted sources. Otherwise, put off big decisions until after the first week of November. With Venus in Virgo, your lover will be a source of support and security and money will pour in. Whatever your goals or dreams be sure to concentrate as concentration is the key to your success. You can achieve anything that you set your mind to and determination and consistency will take you there.

Sagittarius Weekly Horoscope
The sun in Libra brings encouragement and immense support from your life partner. This placement promotes good understanding and synergy between romantic partners creating a period of bliss and unison. For those of you who are single, you may meet someone adventurous, free-thinking and fun and embark on and enjoyable affair. You can expect to receive lucky news and a degree of name and fame. With Mercury in Libra, you will find it easy to complete any tasks that you need to work on so this is a proactive, productive time for you. Venus in Virgo is a wonderful period for the professionals amongst you although it may bring with it a degree of work pressure. When you face pressure, you feel like running away but you do need to learn to stand and face the music at times. The only way to conquer pressure is to face up to it and to admit when things are getting a bit too much. Implementing coping strategies can make a huge difference. As long as you feel free, you can bunker down when needed.

Capricorn Weekly Horoscope
The sun in Libra is pushing you to work harder professionally if you want to get desired results. You need to be prepared for some competition. Although not a competitive person by nature, you do strive for excellence, even perfectionism at times, and you know how to push yourself to conquer whichever mountain you choose to climb. You need to persevere with your willpower as this is the key to your success. Those of you who are single will be in romantic spirits and you may attract a worthy love, perhaps someone at work. Mercury in Libra pushes you to develop at work to ensure that you utilize your skills and focus on key areas that you want to progress in. With Venus in Virgo luck will favour you in all that you do. This should be comforting news dear Capricorn. Be careful not to be influenced into bad behaviours. You may overindulge after long periods of abstinence but it is better to avoid these behaviours.

Aquarius Weekly Horoscope
This week, you enjoy prosperous health and will be relieved of problems that have troubled you in the past. Your love life is in a harmonious and blissful state and it’s possible that marriage may be on the cards. Things are also on the up for you professionally so you are wildly successful and positive in all that you do this week. You are lucky this week in all that you do but you may find that you are lacking interest in religious activities. This is a wonderful time for love so if you are on the lookout for a new partner this is the time to put yourself out there. With Venus in Virgo, you may experience problems with your stomach. This may be related to anxiety or poor diet. Practice stress relief techniques and improve your nutrition. Professionally you need to remain on the ball. Be calm and collected and continue on your chosen course.

Pisces Weekly Horoscope
Dear Pisces, this week you need to ensure that you keep your head down in order to take care of professional tasks at work. It is easy for you to have your head in the clouds and to live in a world of fantasy and this can create wonderful artistic and creative opportunities but ultimately you do need to ground yourself in the real world. You may lack confidence or falter when it comes to making big decisions so you may wish to hold off until mid-November. With Mercury in Libra, you receive appreciation and adulation from society at large and this helps boost your confidence and well-being. Venus in Virgo keeps your personal life smooth and sweet and there is much to look forward to. There is a sense of harmony in your love life and you are receptive to your partner's energy. You want to be close and you enjoy the feeling of being in love. This is a dreamy mystical time in your life and it may feel that all of your deepest dreams are coming true.
