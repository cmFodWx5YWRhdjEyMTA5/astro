Your Monthly Horoscope for July 2018

Expert astrologers of Astroyogi give out the monthly horoscope for July 2018. For personalised horoscope analysis and future insights consult our experts online.

Aries
With the sun in Gemini until the 16th, you effortlessly connect with the younger generation. This enables you to bridge generational gaps, uplift and motivate, but you will learn lessons too. Those younger than us can teach us to reconnect with imagination, curiosity and hope, traits that we often lose in adulthood. You will feel more playful and be more engaged socially. This is the beginning of a busy time for you, but it’s the sort of business you enjoy rather than feel fulfilled to obligate. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer, and you may make hasty career-related decisions due to an innate sense of restlessness. Aries, you are known for being bold and rash, so this should come as no surprise. Do be cautious that you are making decisions for the right reasons. If you focus, there will be no space for anxiety or doubt. Try to channel your cardinal energy in a beneficial direction. With Mercury in Cancer until 2nd September, you enjoy a prosperous period at home. Family and the domestic sphere is immensely rewarding for you. Venus remains in Cancer which signifies change at home. You’ll either want to move or redefine your environment in some way, perhaps by redecorating. Women are a source of happiness, whether romantically, platonically or in terms of familial love. On the 5th July, Venus moves into expansive, show-stopping Leo, which promises good tidings for those of you who are soon to be parents. Children promise happiness. Mars remains in Capricorn can trigger worry and austerity. Capricorn is the father of the zodiac and always exerts caution. Get things in order and you will feel better.

Taurus
The continued impact of the sun in Gemini ensures financial gain and a harmonious personal life. You should feel confident and content. The key to your success is your ability to communicate. If you can exercise patience and assert yourself, you will be understood. You also possess a melodious, soulful voice and it carries you well in any avenue that involves voice work or singing. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer which promotes a certain restlessness of spirit. You may be drawn to taking short trips alone. There may be a degree of anxiety surrounding choices that you are making. You need to have faith in yourself and follow your intuition. With Mercury in Cancer, you may be in a quarrelsome mood. There is a moody energy that you should push towards sport or some goal. Venus remains in Cancer which enables you to fortify bonds with relatives, particularly those younger than you. This is a very promising period in which life rewards you immensely. On the 5th, Venus moves into gregarious, generous Leo, and you’ll want to renovate your home and immediate surroundings. Quality and a touch of elitism appeal. The impact of Mars in industrious Capricorn ensures that you receive good news based on the effort you put in. You’ll be in the mood to expand.

Gemini
With the sun in your own sign of Gemini, you enjoy a period that supports your family life and bolsters your friendships. You feel positive and radiate good vibes and energy. This lends you a sense of warmth and familiarity which encourages others to be around you. This is a wonderful time for socialising. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer indicating monetary profit and a new source of income. With your ingenious, clever mind, you are always able to cook up new ways to make a living. You are keen to experiment. With Mercury in Cancer, you enjoy a profitable period, especially if you work in artistic or political fields. With Venus in Cancer, you are advised to invest some of the money you’ve earnt so that you can put it to work for you. If you’ve loaned money to others, it will be returned to you. Your ability to communicate is never in doubt whether verbally or via the written word. This skill is so unique to Gemini and your fellow air signs Aquarius and Libra, and it is the key to your understanding of life. You come to know yourself through others. Venus moves into Leo on the 5th and you find yourself active, spirited and sociable. Leo enthuses you with the confidence and courage to get life going and you make be bolder, more daring. There is the urge to do. With Mars in Capricorn suggests something new, whether it be a home, career, lover or vehicle. You love novelty, so you welcome it in with open arms.

Cancer
The impact of the sun in Gemini indicates that you need to take care of your health and finances. This isn’t too problematic. You are a rather orderly sign. You simply need to have your head in the right place. If coupled, your partner is a source of support for you even in times of struggle. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer and you may be under some emotional stress. You don’t feel particularly confident in yourself. Cancer has a very sensitive, tempestuous energy. You could liken Cancer’s energy to the ebbing and flowing of the waves of the sea. There are natural ups and downs, and this is simply how your energy expresses itself. You need to be gentle with yourself and understanding the natural rhythms you are privy to. With Mercury in Cancer, there is a restlessness to your nature. Finding a goal to focus yourself on will be helpful as will spiritual practices like meditation and yoga. With Venus in Cancer, there is an urge to travel and marry. You may even marry overseas! You are drawn to what is pleasing to the eye and comfortable. With Venus in Leo on the 5th, you enjoy a financially buoyant time and bond with family and friends. This is a romantic period and you may find a new partner or find that your relationship enjoys a new lease of life. With Mars in Capricorn, your inner playfulness emerges, and you spend time with young siblings. You may worry about the future but keep yourself grounded on the now. Meditation will help here.

Leo
This is a great period for you Leo. It gets off to a good start with the sun in Gemini. You are lucky in all that you do and may become famous, if so inclined. Your hard work helps push you to new heights and you achieve more than you ever thought possible. You are also supported by those who are in a position to support you, either with money or connections. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer and you may feel a lack of fundamental self-confidence. You don’t believe in yourself. You may also be susceptible to minor health issues. As a result, you may lash out and draw on your infamous Leo temper. Positive outlets for such anger include sports and anything competitive or performative in nature. You need a valve for your passion. With Mercury in Cancer, you need to focus on keeping your mind clear, stable and focused. How you do that is dependent on your personality and temperament, but anything artistic or physical should assist. Venus in Cancer brings new challenges in the workplace, but you are able to rise to the occasion. Venus moves into Leo on the 5th and you may feel a lack of love in life. Remember that the love for the self is very important. Practice self-love and self-acceptance. Embrace yourself as you are. With Mars in Capricorn, you are working towards a goal and methodically climbing the mountain. You will see such wonders at the top, but make sure you enjoy the journey too.

Virgo
Your career enjoys a boost this month, and you may wish to either change jobs or expand a business you own. You have itchy feet at work and maintaining the path you are on just isn’t enough to satisfy you. You want something more than that. Make sure that you aren’t being too much of a perfectionist. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer and you feel settled and secure in your own skin. In fact, you shine. It can take a lot for Virgo natives to feel truly comfortable in who they are, so this is a period in which you will excel. If only you could bottle this feeling! With Mercury in Cancer, you enjoy excellent health and financial gain. There are also plenty of new opportunities for you to grab. With Venus in Cancer, you are drawn to sharpening your mind and wits and pursuing new educational opportunities feels natural to you. The elder generation help you understand your place in the universe. Life lessons learnt at this time are valuable. On the 5th, Venus moves into Leo which encourages you to be gentler with yourself. Leo energy is a bit fiery and full on for a sign as meticulous as you are. Mars in Capricorn can produce an uncomfortable energy, but it is through this energy that we grow. You may lack trust in yourself and others. Do whatever you can to fortify this. Avoid bad company.

Libra
Lucky Libra, with the sun in Gemini, an overarching sense of luck supports all that you do. You are drawn to the spiritual realm. It is healing for you to connect to it as often as possible. Art also has a therapeutic effect. Visiting galleries or beautiful agricultural landmarks will do you the world of good. Things are also flourishing romantically which always keeps a Libra smiling. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer, which promises a good period for professionals although hard work will be required. Those senior to you will support your ambitions if you wish to climb the ladder, so be open about your aspirations. Mercury in Cancer suggests that a combination of luck and hard work will propel you to great heights. You may even visit a famous landmark for work. With Venus in Cancer, you shine in the workplace and maintain a calm and composed attitude. You are the eye of the storm, centred even when everything around you is in a state of flux. You will make sound, responsible decisions both at work and in your private life. With Venus moving into Leo on the 5th, you enjoy an excellent stage of life. This is a particularly good time for you financially. If single, love is close by. With Mars in Capricorn, you need to keep an eye on your health and finances. Be prudent and get planning.

Scorpio
You are naturally in tune with family and tradition, even if you are a rebel at heart. You value family and are powerfully sentimental. Your love and loyalty know no bounds. This can lead to worry as you stress about the wellbeing of family and friends. Try not to focus on what you cannot change. Focus only on what you can do. You may feel sluggish emotionally and low in self-confidence. Spend more time with your partner. Their love will do you the world of good. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer, initiating a favourable phase for you. You feel in love with life and enthusiastic about what is around the corner. You have a sense of perspective and expect good things to happen. With Mercury in Cancer you make feel tense at work so take time to carve out some space for your needs. With Venus in Cancer, your personal life is good. Marital life is blissful and new marriages are blessed. On the 5th, Venus moves into Leo, which suggests excitement and a draw towards romance. Mars in Capricorn ensures that your life partner supports you. You may feel connected to them on a profound level. Money finds its way to you and a new family member or baby brings much joy. If a student, you will need to focus a bit harder to ensure that you get the results you long for. You have it in you to achieve great things.

Sagittarius
Life always brings challenges and you know this by now. What makes these challenges easier to weather is being possessed of a certain optimism and buoyancy of spirit. No matter what gets you down, you’ll always get back up. Understand that this month, whatever comes your way, you’ll not only survive but thrive. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer and you’ll need to be more focused especially at work. This placement produces a mixed impact on your personal life so turn to meditation and exercise for balance and as an outlet. Mercury in Cancer continues to bring you unexpected monetary gain. Do make sure you squirrel some away for a rainy day. With Venus in Cancer, you should take time to prioritise your health. You are a rambunctious sign and much like Aries natives, may endure a few scrapes and bruises in your pursuit of fun and frivolity. Your positive approach to life can bring a good spin to health and wellbeing if you let it. With Venus moving into Leo on the 5th, you experience good fortune and life is full of harmony and prosperity. This is a good period for you in every aspect of life. Mars in Capricorn can bring pressure, but you perform well at work and impress those around you.

Capricorn
Capricorn, you are always a winner and very self-assured of this fact. You understand that victory is guaranteed because you have a realistic, practical approach to life and tasks. You understand your duties and responsibilities and rise to the challenge as required. You also set tall expectations for yourself and grow to meet them. There is a tenacity and quiet determination to you that gets you through almost anything. With the sun in Gemini, you continue to meet these challenges head-on. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer which is a wonderful period for work and business. Your own health and private life are also good. With Mercury in Cancer, luck gradually builds and begins to favour you with time. Others may envy you as you simply seem to have it all, and how do you remain so composed? You understand that you work hard and deserve your success and that you are much like a duck, who seems to float on the surface of the water with ease, whilst your feet kick frantically beneath! With Venus in Cancer, you enjoy a lovely and beneficial impact on your personal life. You live like a true leader, with courage and conviction. On the 5th, Venus moves into Leo causing you to lack excitement. You may feel on autopilot. Take time to do things you relish. Factor in time for treats. These rewards will get you through. Mars in Capricorn urges you to stay away from anything or anyone that may be a bad influence. Avoid drinking, smoking, gossip and seedy behaviours. You are a being of excellence, but your desire to let off steam can lead you down a troubling path. Focus on your goals instead.

Aquarius
This month gets off to a good start, largely due to the impact of the sun in Gemini which promises a glimmer of fame and recognition. If studying, this placement gives you a quick, keen and agile brain. You communicate brilliantly and can easily persuade others. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer, ensuring that you excel at work. You are a natural leader; detached enough to see the bigger picture, tenacious enough to take the rough with the smooth and warm enough to have a loyal following. A sense of humour doesn’t hurt either! With Mercury in Cancer, you excel at writing, perfect if you are a blogger, writer or author. This avenue may generate wealth or fame. Venus in Cancer keeps you lucky and this luck seems to run on a course of its own. It’s important that you avoid conflicts and quarrels. Drama of any kind should be avoided. With Venus moving into Leo on the 5th, you enjoy an excellent period professionally and your personal life is bliss to boot. Everything feels right in the world and you are deeply content. Embrace this period as it is set to last until October. Regardless of whatever else may happen in your life, you feel able to weather it and manage it due to this growing sense of calm. Mars in Capricorn can suggest betrayal in your love life but use your gut instinct as your compass and approach situations with diplomacy.

Pisces
As a water sign, your self-confidence and opinion of yourself tend to waver, much as waves to ebb and flow. Nonetheless, you see things beginning to happen and manifest in your life, and this should give you some degree of confidence. You feel mellow and want to spend time with family. You want to feel cosy and comfortable and your home feels like a safe retreat, as it should. On the 16th, the sun moves into Cancer, and unexpected good news comes your way. There may be a new development that you had put into place a while ago but forgotten about since. Funny isn’t it how these things come back around? You are brimming with innovation and creativity and this manifests in all areas of life. With Venus in Cancer, you bring your innovation to work where you are noticed. Yes, you may need to raise your head above the parapet. If coupled, your mind may drift towards marriage. You want to cement a longstanding bond by making a lifelong commitment. With Venus in Leo on the 5th, you should watch your sugar intake and take care of your stomach and liver. Avoid fatty foods and alcohol. Turn your attention to your love life, which may need to be rekindled. With Mars in Capricorn, life feels smooth and effortless. This is a lovely placement for appreciating what you have and expressing gratitude.
