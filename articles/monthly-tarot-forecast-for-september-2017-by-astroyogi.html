Monthly Tarot Forecast For September 2017 by astroYogi

Celebrity astrologer Mita Bhan unfolds Tarot Readings for the month of September 2017. Read on to find out what is in store for your sign this month. Enjoy your reading.

Aries

Phew! A crisis may have just been averted by smart thinking. Or you could come out of a situation feeling like it could have been much worse. Time to organise yourself and make sure everything is in place and in working order. Avoid delaying your commitments. And ensure you live up to every promise. Even in your personal life, you may find yourself re-evaluating relationships. Perhaps you’ve given more than you receive, or you feel drained by a few toxic individuals. Time for pruning don’t you think? Financially, money flows in but you feel like saving it for a rainy day, or better still investing the money for days ahead.

Taurus

If things aren't going your way, why are you repeating the same process again and again? Time to walk away, or change your game plan completely. Remember, the only person under your control is yourself. The others are going to behave and do things exactly the way they’ve always done. It’s pointless to fret over spilled milk, just pick up the pieces and walk away. You may hear about a divorce or a break up of a loved one and may lend a shoulder. A recent friendship may deepen as trust begins to develop. A boss may take you into confidence, or you could find yourself involved in a highly confidential project. You’re good with keeping secrets and people begin to trust you. Watch out for over exertion, you could sprain a limb. Moderation is the key.

Gemini

If you’ve been dilly dallying about something, this is the month where you’ll make a few key decisions in your life. Lately, you may have allowed yourself to go adrift and its time to catch the reins all over again. Set goals about what you want exactly, and make a strategy and stick to it. Your energy levels are high, bordering on irritable. Just realize, not everybody has your quick mind, and not everybody can match your current levels of energy. You could recover old debts and may refuse someone who wants to borrow off you. Friends and family come to the forefront in a reunion or a farewell. Someone could move far away. With all this busy-ness make sure you get the time out for yourself to recharge your batteries. Pay attention to when you’ve had enough.

Cancer

Things slowly begin to get better. And you begin to adopt a more flexible approach to your life. The lesser your expectations, the more the pleasant surprises. You could find yourself juggling expenses and trying to find a balance between yourself and your work, or your work and your family. Another source of income begins to open up, and you may consider flexitime options, or freelancing or starting your own consultancy. Business owners may work on diversification or introducing a new product or service. Remember, the key to success lies in how well you organize yourself. Poor planning could result in a minor disaster or just plain misunderstandings. Be wary about those who gossip around you, they could be discussing you behind your back as well. All in all, a good month for trying something new.

Leo

Travel plans may get finalized or you may find yourself packing your bags at a moment’s notice. If there was tension earlier, this month proves to bring a little peace of mind. When you learn there are things you cannot change and simply have to accept, you relax with the flow. Even in your relationships, you learn that letting go of something that isn't working will lead to a better situation. If there was conflict or tension in a relationship, things slowly get better.  Use your intuition to guide you about the people in your life. Sometimes, it is better to let things be, rather than ruffle feathers. An old friend may reach out to you or you may travel to meet with old acquaintances. A good month for introspection and seeing how far you have come.

Virgo

It’s all about moods this month. Managing your own, or having to deal with a moody man in your life. Remember, suppressing a feeling isn't the way, acknowledge whatever you feel, let it go and carry on. Positive news for those involved with home based businesses, small start ups and consultancy. Money that was owed to you will be repaid. And if you were planning to make a substantial purchase, this month will see you finalizing a deal or shortlisting exactly what you want. Be a little more sensitive to those in need, lend a shoulder or an ear to someone in trouble.  An annoying man will have to be dealt with emotional maturity and diplomacy. Treat your body with care and compassion. Don’t ignore symptoms and if you’d like, embark on a long term health care/fitness plan for your future. A good month to express your feelings of love.

Libra

Someone around you isn't being honest and you may be taken for a ride if you’re not careful. Before you blindly trust someone, check with your gut feeling. At the workplace, someone’s excuses may delay the project. Stop being so forgiving of others and playing the good person. Time to assert your authority and take charge of the situation. People may be taking advantage of your silence, assuming it's okay. Speak out and define boundaries. A month to be patient with plans as delays, modifications and reworks may dominate the weeks ahead. Financially things to be on a slow but not for long. Keep your spirits up and don’t give up. Family matters are harmonious and a loved one may extend love or a gift your way. Single Librans could find themselves in a relationship that isn't going anywhere. Time to turn inwards and ask yourself if the person is truly sincere. Don’t judge a book by its cover.

Scorpio

Everything must change and the sooner you adapt, the better your life gets. Face whatever you fear and realize that it’s not coming true. Things are never quite as bad as they appear. You may need to work on keeping anxiety levels down especially at the work place. Deadlines may loom and crises may suddenly occur, but you must understand, nothing is quite as critical as you are making it out to be. Family brings solace your way. And you may choose to withdraw into the comfort of your home, preferring your own company. Domestic expenses may take you by surprise towards the end of the month, and if you were planning on buying that new car, you may do it sooner than you think. Avoid over exertion, pay attention to the signals your body gives you. When you’ve had enough, take a break.

Sagittarius

After a point, you realise you can't please everyone. And those who complain, or criticise you behind your back will continue to do so regardless of what you say or do. The card of detachment urges you to break free from relationships that are toxic. Let go of unpleasant memories. And move on. You may choose to leave a dead end job in the weeks ahead. Don’t feel guilty, you’ve done your best. Healthwise, a month for taking extra care of yourself. Make sure you boost your immunity and don’t neglect symptoms. An old friend from the past may contact you out of the blue. And if you were planning to travel, you could finalise plans in the days ahead. Financially a good month, you may discover some money tucked away. Trust your gut about something. Remember, when one door closes, another one opens. Always.

Capricorn

The worst is over. And a tiny ray of hope begins to emerge. A wonderful period for those who’ve been ill, or have been struggling with a stressful situation. The cards foretell gradual recovery and soon, the start of a new and positive chapter in your life. Stop obsessing about things that are beyond your control. Don’t believe your negative thoughts. Meditation, alternative therapy or all forms of de-stressing are highlighted this month as you literally recharge your own batteries. At work, a crisis gets averted in the nick of time. Financially, a period of windfalls and sudden gains is appearing. Just remember to believe in yourself, if there’s anything that’s your biggest enemy, it’s your self-doubt.

Aquarius

Excess baggage is weighing you down. Let go of the unnecessary. Stop holding on to the past. And disconnect from things that no longer serve your purpose. This month is all about freedom. But how can you experience it, if you’re going to keep holding on? In personal relationships, jealousy and possessiveness come in the way of harmony. An older woman may cause stress in the family. And if single, you may have to contend with a third party, vying for attention.A good month to spend more time outdoors, the cards urge you to connect with Nature and listen to your inner voice. Work matters go slow and steady and a client may act difficult. Remember, in the long run, none of this will matter. There’s nothing as precious as peace of mind. And the Universe wants you to find it.

Pisces

You get what you give. And there’s no escaping the Law of Karma. Past efforts will be rewarded or penalized. The weeks ahead will bring forth events and people from your past, for one last karmic clearance so be prepared. A good period to forgive those who have hurt you, or forgive yourself for mistakes you may have made. Workwise after a bumpy start to September, things begin to settle down. A huge project gets you appreciation and recognition. Focus on immediate practical step-by step details. You could reorganize a job into smaller tasks. Not a month to take it easy by any standards, especially if you’re hoping for that promotion. In love matters, a new person could catch your fancy. Or you could find yourself attracted to someone who isn't your usual type. Family matters remain cordial. And if you’ve been splurging a lot lately, how about tightening the purse strings a little?
