Prabhas - Astro Analysis of the Bahubali Star

Venkata Satyanarayana Prabhas Raju Uppalapati is as big in the Tollywood ‘market’ as his name. Popularly known as Prabhas, this Indian actor rose to a fame that very few actors have managed to reach, after the release of his epic film, Baahubali: The Beginning.

Born on 23rd October, Prabhas is a passionate, ambitious, committed and empathetic Scorpio.

The typical Scorpio is a very private person and in spite of being such a celebrity, Prabhas keeps his circle of friends close. He even prefers working with those who he has known for a certain number of years.

A true Scorpio in every sense, Prabhas is intensely loyal to his family and friends, and they have always been more important to him than money. He is very close to his mother and enjoys spending quality time with her. Prabhas kept in touch with his childhood friends even after becoming a superstar, and one of his closest ‘bumchum’ manages his work for him.

Ruled by both Mars and Pluto, this Scorpion is undoubtedly masculine and assertive. While Mars, the God of War, has made Prabhas seek an identity for himself in Tollywood; Pluto, the God of the Underworld, has given Prabhas a heightened sense of intuition. It is this intuition that makes him believe in fated connections and destined love.

This Scorpio is not one to settle for less than what he knows is right for him. The Water Sign is capable of incredible compassion and emotional understanding, therefore, Scorpios love with all their heart. Although romantic link-ups with his Baahubali co-star, Anushka Shetty, have been rife since the release of the film, Prabhas is not one to publicize his emotions. His fans are waiting with bated breath for a special announcement of his betrothal and are very excited to know about the lucky girl who would be chosen by the actor as his life partner.

An intelligent man, Prabhas, is good at managing his finances. Although he went through a bad phase financially just before Baahubali was released, he still did not succumb to offers of money that other directors tried to lure him with.

Natives belonging to the fixed sign of Scorpio can be stubborn and the director of Prabhas’s latest film, Saaho, can vouch for this. Many a time Prabhas does what he wants to while shooting for the film, in spite of being given instructions to do otherwise.

A Scorpio is highly competitive and will go out of the way to be the best in his field. Baahubali’s success has made Prabhas realize that to be at the top, one has to work hard. He has undergone rigorous training sessions to pull off many stunts by himself, for his action-packed film Saaho that counts as one of the most expensive Indian films. 

As he celebrates his birthday in 2019, the stars will align in Prabhas’s favour and with a little effort, he should be able to witness breakthroughs in various areas of his life. Jupiter’s transit to Scorpio in the final quarter of the year will help Prabhas in his professional life. Mars’ transit to Pisces, just before the end of 2019 will make Prabhas taste the sweet flavour of success.

On his birthday, Astroyogi wishes him all the best for his mega film, Jaan, to be released in 2020.
