Your Weekly Horoscope – 28 May to 3 June 2018

Your weekly horoscope (28th May to 3rd June, 2018) analysis can provide insights on the crucial planetary movements and its impact on your life. Stay tuned to Astroyogi’s expert astrologers for horoscope analysis.

Aries

The sun in Taurus ensures that you are industrious at work and continue to perform well. Those senior to you admire your work ethic and spunk. You work hard but you also have that certain something that encourages others to respond warmly to you, and this serves you well. In love, make your voice heard but be compassionate and kind. This yields the best results. Mercury in Taurus keeps your mind calm and peaceful. This is the perfect time to solidify meditative practices as you are so ready for it. This calmness makes it easier for you to see what you want. The path becomes clearer. You continue to connect to the younger generation and may make plans to expand your business overseas.

Taurus

You are methodical at work and know how to get things done but do make sure that this approach doesn’t cause you to move slowly. Work smart and you can avoid over-working! Your powerful ability to communicate as well as your zest for life makes you personally popular and people enjoy being around you. Your social life is full, and you may have no space left in your calendar. If single, you may attract a new love as you are irresistible to the opposite sex now, largely due to your personality. Ensure that you don’t hurt anyone’s feelings. With Venus in Gemini, you are witty, talkative and enchanting. You come up with innovative ideas that might be beneficial from a financial perspective.

Gemini

You have grown so much recently Gemini. If only we could bottle confidence and drink it at whim. You may not feel this confident forever, but this week, you remain so and as such, you can do whatever you put your mind to. This is a truly brilliant time for you and you are grateful for it. Even if nothing special is happening, you feel so amazingly good. In love, you bring an endearing sense of enthusiasm which pierces through the harshest armour. Your desire for travel continues to be strong and you should make some plans to move. Whether single or attached, your love life is fantastic. All the best of your Gemini nature is rising to the fore.

Cancer

Are you working too hard? It’s okay if this is what you need to do right now but do take some time to rest, eat well, meditate and have fun as this will serve you well and do you the world of good. You have a taste for success and may desire to move up the ladder. There are so many new opportunities presenting themselves to you that you are in a position to pick and choose which promotes great freedom. Your mind is calm and still, like tranquil water and you see clearer than you have in some time. Life feels harmonious and you are confident about your purpose. Make sure that you continue to take some time for yourself.

Leo

Life continues to tick along beautifully for Leo natives. All areas of life are imbued with luck, light and love. You feel confident and sure of your purpose and mission. A renewed and awakened interest in spiritually buoys you up and keeps you on the right path. You are a light to friends and family and are personally popular and well-liked, which serves you well at work and at play. You are constantly making new friends as you are saying yes to new experiences and there are endless options for you. You simply need to follow your instincts and pick the path that appeals. There really is no wrong answer as long as you are happy. Listen to that inner voice and you will not go wrong.

Virgo

Recently, you have been urged to consider changing your environment to one that is more cohesive to good health. Only you will know what that means to you, but your picture might be being close to water or in a place with less people or even in the mountains. Whatever picture you get, try to honour it as this will be powerfully healing for you. This might help you to understand if you require a permanent change of environment or if a temporary retreat is all that you need. Your partner supports you this week and through this, you have the willpower, tenacity and dedication to push forward. All will be well dear Virgo so just hang on in there. You are growing as a person every day.

Libra

Libra, avoid making any big life decisions this week. You can day dream, plan and jot things down, but don’t officially set anything in motion. Simply let your mind run wild. You will be drawn to planning a big trip for a change of scenery and you can have plenty of fun thinking through all the options. Although you are a lucky sign, don’t rely solely on your luck as hard work is also required and luck is not always consistent. It ebbs and flows like life’s rivers. Life is forever a balancing act and achieving this sense of equilibrium is vital to Libra natives, who are represented by the scales. To achieve balance is your life quest. Every day, you move closer to achieving the ideal. The most important thing to learn to balance is yourself, your emotions, your thoughts, your body and your dreams.

Scorpio

You are in the midst of a period of great change and the best thing to do is roll with it. These changes might be of your own making or they might be occurring naturally. All areas of your life feel prosperous although you may experience a few minor setbacks. The key word to focus on is ‘minor.’ There won’t be any heavy heartache here. Your mind may become somewhat unfocused and you won’t feel as settled in yourself as you usually do. Meditation and affirmations will be hugely impactful. Anxieties surrounding your partner may manifest and can encourage depression. Fortunately, Scorpio is a transcendental sign and growth is encouraged when you encounter difficulties. You will rise again like the phoenix. Even if you feel low, on the other end of the spectrum, is the high.

Sagittarius

With the sun in Taurus, you continue to feel in good spirits and perfectly content. All areas of life are ticking along nicely, and you feel plentiful and grateful for your blessings. You also feel very lucky, and this is a natural state for Sagittarius natives. Your jolly, expansive nature encourages joy and merriment. The luckier you feel, the more luck you generate for yourself and others around you, drawing people to wanting to spend time with you. The perfect motto for you is ‘slow and steady wins the race.’ You can slow down, just don’t stop. Your dreams will then come to fruition. With Venus in Gemini, you continue to support your life partner. You can learn much from Gemini, which is your opposite sign, so take heed of what this sign tries to teach you. 

Capricorn

Change is underfoot, and you are learning to embrace it. This is a good lesson for Capricorn natives. Close friends will be immensely supportive of your endeavours and your personal life will feel problem free. This may make you feel somewhat unfocused, as you work well when you have a problem or something to conquer, especially one that you can steadily work towards. You may make problems just for something to solve. If you put your mind towards a goal, project or hobby, especially one that is all-consuming or enables a state of flow, your particular type of energy will feel better utilised. You think of the future, especially in relation to family and children but the most important time is now.

Aquarius

You continue to be in a highly proactive mood and this is an exciting and enjoyable time for you. The level of enthusiasm that you bring to the table is infectious and others may be inspired to go for their own goals with you providing such a strong example. You may not think of yourself as a natural leader, but you are a humanitarian and your interest in human beings, even if at times detached and aloof, can make you a leading force for change. You are good at galvanising people, even if you tend to see things in black and white rather than in shades of grey. You are truly living the life right now and people are encouraged by your cheer, sense of joy and ambitious attitude. Enjoy where you are because this is the very best of life.

Pisces

You feel hugely self-confident this week which sets you off to a good start. All areas of life are beautiful including love, travel and your career, although you may desire setting off in a new direction and jumping onto a new career path. This is a wonderful time for romance so whether single or coupled, you can enjoy plenty of romantic high jinks. You will hit a period part way through the week where you feel somewhat lazy and unmotivated, but you can resolve this by reminding yourself of what you want to achieve and why it is important for you. You need to give yourself a kickstart periodically. Developments continue to happen in your life at a slow and steady pace and travel is indicated.
