Your Weekly Horoscope – April 23rd to April 29th, 2018

Your weekly horoscope (23rd April to 29th April, 2018) analysis can provide insights on the crucial planetary movements and its impact on your life. Stay tuned to Astroyogi’s expert astrologers for horoscope analysis.


Aries

This is a good week to build on anything you have set in motion in recent weeks. Whatever you have put in place, now has an opportunity to grow. If you are writing a novel, allow it to reach fruition. If you are working out, look at the results you have achieved and keep pushing. If you are trying for a promotion at work, put the finishing touches on your offer. You have the confidence and focus to do anything you desire. Yoga and meditation can supercharge this even more. You are in a romantic phase and may be in the market for marriage. If you have a long-term partner with whom you are compatible and happy, you may want to think about deepening your commitment to one another. With Venus in Taurus, love and pleasure are at the forefront of your mind. Try to balance the requirements for work and play.

Taurus

The sun in Aries brings you good things and also encourages you to go out and get things done. Taurus and Aries energies are very familiar and beneficial for one another. Taurus can bring perseverance to Aries and Aries can lend Taurus active fire with which to have the confidence and gusto to get things done. Your family are also supportive of all that you do which is naturally meaningful to you. You bring a sense of urgency to work which enables you to excel and complete tasks quickly. With Mercury in Aquarius, seniors notice your output at work and are impressed with your determination and drive to succeed. These are admirable qualities and they go over well. Venus in Taurus keeps love spellbinding and tender and also draws others to you with ease. At work, people are becoming interested in you as a true contender.  

Gemini

Your high spirits are set to continue this week. What a wonderful week to be a Gemini! You are truly on form! This overabundance of energy may push you to do far more than you ordinarily would. You simply want to live, to be in the world and soak it all up. In many ways, this is what the Gemini is designed for. You are the eternally curious child and to live and flourish is so important to you. Retain this sense of playfulness and you can’t go far wrong. With Venus in Taurus, you are apt to be a tad more sensible about love, but you also want to connect on a meaningful level. Your attraction to someone may be less superficial and based more on personality, character and merit. You are looking for an enduring love and you may just have found it! 

Cancer

At work, you have ever-increasing chances of moving up the ladder thanks to your commitment, strength, tenacity and enthusiasm. You are a joy to be around and people want you as part of the party. There are so many opportunities coming your way that it’s simply a matter of knowing what to pick. You are at a point in life where you are able to be somewhat selective. With Venus in Taurus, you need to make sure that your health is a key priority. Water revives water signs so maybe a break close to the sea will do you good. Listen to your intuition and to your instincts. Don’t be afraid to follow them to wherever they may guide you. This is not the time to make dramatic, life-changing decisions. Instead, mull over your options and bask in the love and unending support from your partner.

Leo

The sun remains in Aries and as such, you continue to be in high spirits. You are on top Leo form which ensures that you are a kind, loyal, wise and generous leader, but there is no mistaking, you must be first! You do all this with a sense of warmth which ensures that you come across well. Your interest in spirituality blossoms and deepens into something that brings you tremendous satisfaction and courage. This is a way in which you can learn to know yourself better and in a more intimate way. Health should still be a priority for you. It is easy for you to see yourself as naturally strong, fit and capable but health is something that must be nurtured, or it can fall away. In love, things are developing in an exciting fashion. Enjoy what life has to offer you.

Virgo

Health is wealth’ as the old saying goes, and your health must continue to be your focus. It may be that you need to make some big changes, but smaller changes naturally build to make a difference. There are common knowledge habits that you may need to be reminded of. Firstly, find an environment that is cohesive to healing and winding down. Secondly, eat well, drink well, sleep well. Thirdly, ensure that you speak with a doctor if you have any health concerns. Fourthly, find ways to calm your mind and emotions as they can create tension and ill health in the body. Please do keep up with yoga and meditation. Make these a regular part of your week. Remember not to be deterred by those who bring negativity into your life. Focus on being positive and strong.

 Libra

This week, you may feel a bit stagnant. Don’t be afraid to sit down, think things through and figure out where to go next. There is no rush and planning is key. You are urged not to make any big decisions this week. This is more the time for mulling things over, although a big, rejuvenating trip will do you the world of good. Meditation and yoga should continue to be utilised for their fruitful results. If you have not already changed your environment, please do this as this will have a tremendous impact on your well-being. In love, things may be becoming more serious and marriage and parenthood are indicated. Likewise, there may be issues in your relationship which need to be addressed before the next step is taken.

 Scorpio

The sun continues in Aries and as such, life may take an unexpected turn, but this is nothing to worry about. It could be a lottery win, new baby, new job or new love. The options are endless. All you need to do is ensure that you embrace the newness with both hands. Be open when the opportunities arise. This is a great time for mothers. If you are a mother, your bond is solidified with your children. If not a mother, your bond with your own mother or maternal figure is fortified. This is one of the most powerful and special bonds and this cannot be denied. Family, history and roots are important to Scorpio natives even if they tend towards unconventional behaviour themselves at times.

 Sagittarius

When we feel that fate is on our side, we can feel invincible. You are deeply in touch with your intuition right now and able to make decisions which benefit your life and that feel right. Yoga and meditation will further deepen this connection should you wish to explore it further, and things of a spiritual nature matter to you right now. Your sign is already innately connected to spirituality. It’s a way for you to connect to yourself and others, but right now, this takes on a more profound meaning. With Venus in Taurus, you long to travel and these trips may take on a spiritual significance. You may wish to stay in an ashram or go on a yoga holiday. Listen to your heart.

 Capricorn

With the sun in Aries, change is coming your way thick and fast and there may be a period of adjustment as you figure out all of this novelty. A traditionalist, you prefer things to move at a slow and steady pace, but sometimes change is more like a hurricane and learning to adapt is a vital lesson. As all of this change is positive, it should not be too difficult for you but nonetheless, change can be somewhat unsettling. You may simply need to readjust your sails. Fortunately, close friends support you in all areas of your life and you know you can turn to them. Usually a workaholic, you may struggle to focus right now. With Venus in Taurus, you feel serene and happy. Take the time to enjoy this energy by embracing the quieter elements of life.

 Aquarius

The impact of Aries can never be underestimated. As the first sign of the zodiac, it is the sign to yell ‘go!’ With this in mind, you are in a go-getting mood. You want to travel, see friends, be with your lover, care for your family, get your career going and do all that you need to do. This is a great energy and a wonderful time to use it. This is a truly invincible time for you. It feels like things move out of your way, giving you a clear road to the top. With Venus in Taurus, you get the conclusions you’ve longed for. There is a sense that certain things are reaching a long-overdue end, and this brings relief to you. In love, the opportunities are endless, but you are enjoying a wonderful relationship that seems to tick all boxes.

Pisces

As April dwindles to a close, you remain in a strong position, particularly in terms of your confidence. Often in life, you may lack confidence and need others to draw you out of your shell. Well, this is not the case this week. You go into May thinking about your career, love life and personal goals and you have the strength to make some big changes happen if you wish to. You will desire to change and you may need to think through what kind of change is required. With Venus in Taurus, you may spend more than you need to so try to think before you splash the cash. You feel calm and peaceful and this is a great note to end the month on, as you sit in contentment, looking forward to the next chapter of life. Remember, you are the author of your own destiny.
